# A Free Alternative to PhotoSplit That Works on Any Device

PhotoSplit is great — until you hit the subscription screen.

**GridSnap** does the same thing for free: split a photo into a grid of tiles, post them to Instagram in order, and viewers swipe left to see the full image. No install, no account, no uploads.

Open https://kingson4wu.github.io/GridSnap/ in Safari, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen", and it works like a native app — full-screen, offline-capable, icon on your home screen. On Android, Chrome prompts you to install automatically.

**What it supports:** 6 grid layouts (3×3, 2×2, 2×3, 1×2, 1×3, 1×4), 4 aspect ratios, pinch-to-zoom crop, rotation, 1080px output. Photos never leave your device.

The one thing PhotoSplit still has going for it is native app polish. For everything else, GridSnap covers it — and it runs on Android and desktop too.

**GitHub:** https://github.com/Kingson4Wu/GridSnap
